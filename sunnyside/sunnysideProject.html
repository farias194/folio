Sunnyside Hoboken was a cafe located in downtown Hoboken, offering all day breakfast for commuters and office workers for the NJ/NYC metro area. They had successful reviews and were looking to expand their digital footprint with a website that would inform customers of their menu and drink options. Having a laid back casual vibe, the design for the website features muted pastel colors with a simple top down structure that allows users to easily and quickly find menu information..
